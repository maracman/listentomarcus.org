Marcus Anderson is a classically trained, Sydney based composer, specialising in composing both contemporary and classical styles of music. He is a professional performer and a multi instrumentalist. He is a graduate of the Sydney Conservatorium of music specialising in voice in addition to being a proficient pianist, guitar player and bassist (he also dabbles in drums, clarinet, and whatever else he can get his hands on)

Marcus got his start in composing after being awarded best composer at the SFS film festival in 2014, his body of work includes scoring for television adverts, web series and dozens of short films. He has worked and collaborated with multiple international artists in addition to writing and producing his own music. Marcus loves the challenge of doing new things within music production and composition and is driven by a philosophy of continually learning and expanding his craft.
